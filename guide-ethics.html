Ethics Application Preparation Assistant

User Guide for Testing

⚠ Important — this is a prototype
This tool is in active development and has not yet been independently validated. Always apply your own professional judgment. Your feedback is what will improve it.

What this tool does

The Ethics Application Preparation Assistant guides WMS staff and students through the preparation stage before submitting a research ethics application. It identifies the correct form and the documents required. It does not approve research, complete forms, or submit applications — it handles the navigation, not the content.

Before you start

You do not need to prepare anything in advance. The tool collects everything through its questions. It helps to have a rough sense of your project type — e.g. MBA research, PhD preliminary, staff research, or a course application.

How it works — step by step

  1. Open the tool and answer the first question. It will ask what type of project you are preparing an ethics application for. Select the option that best matches.
  2. Follow the decision pathway. The tool asks one question at a time and routes you based on your answers — whether your project collects data from people, whether participants are under 16, and so on.
  3. Receive your checklist. At the end, the tool tells you which ethics form to use and which supporting documents to prepare before entering the ethics portal.
  4. If your situation is unclear, the tool will direct you to the WMS Ethics Committee rather than guessing. Follow that advice.

What to watch for

  • Did the tool correctly identify your project type and route you to the right form?
  • Were the document requirements accurate and complete?
  • Was any step confusing or unclear?
  • Did the tool ever suggest something that seemed wrong — if so, what?

How to give feedback on the tool

After testing, please note:

  • Whether the routing matched your actual situation
  • Any step where the tool felt uncertain or unclear
  • Whether the final checklist was accurate

Send your feedback directly to Diego Carretero or use the feedback form on the WMS AI Hub.
